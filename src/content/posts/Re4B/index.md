# Resident Evil 4 Remake (RE4B) - Complete Guide

Resident Evil 4 Remake (often abbreviated as RE4B) is Capcom's 2023 reimagining of the 2005 classic. This guide covers everything you need to know.

## Overview

- **Developer**: Capcom
- **Release Date**: March 24, 2023
- **Platforms**: PS5, PS4, Xbox Series X/S, PC
- **Engine**: RE Engine

## Key Changes from Original

### Combat System
- **Parry System** - Press L1/LB at the right moment to block and counter
- **Knife Durability** - Knives now break; manage resources carefully
- **Stealth Kills** - Sneak up on enemies for instant takedowns

### Movement & Controls
- **Over-the-shoulder camera** refined with modern movement
- **Quick turn** (back + circle/B) essential for survival
- **Aim while moving** - No more stationary shooting

## Essential Tips

1. **Conserve Ammo** - Headshots critical; use knife for weak enemies
2. **Upgrade Strategically** - Focus on firepower and capacity first
3. **Sell Old Weapons** - Don't hoard; upgrade one weapon per category
4. **Treasure Maps** - Buy from merchant; reveal all collectibles
5. **Blue Medallions** - Shoot all 16 for special reward

## Weapon Recommendations

| Category | Best Choice | Why |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| Handgun | Red9 | Highest damage, great with stock |
| Shotgun | Striker | High capacity, fast fire rate |
| Rifle | SR M1903 | Best stats overall |
| Magnum | Broken Butterfly | Massive damage for bosses |

## Merchant Locations

The Merchant appears at fixed locations throughout each chapter. Look for blue torches. He offers:
- Weapon upgrades
- Treasure maps
- Attache case upgrades
- Charms (provide passive bonuses)

## Difficulty Modes

- **Assisted** - Auto-aim, more resources, easier parry
- **Standard** - Balanced experience
- **Hardcore** - Original RE4 difficulty, limited saves
- **Professional** - Unlock after Hardcore; permadeath mode

## Post-Game Content

- **New Game+** - Keep all upgrades, weapons, money
- **Mercenaries** - Time-attack mode (free DLC)
- **Separate Ways** - Ada Wong campaign (paid DLC)
- **Assignment Ada** - Short missions

## Verdict

RE4B successfully modernizes a masterpiece while preserving its soul. The parry system adds depth, visuals are stunning, and pacing remains near-perfect. **9.5/10**
